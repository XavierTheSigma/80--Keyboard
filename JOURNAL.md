# Case
I Finally started work on the Case for the keyboard. I did research and I decided to self threaded the holes using the screws. I chose this option because I wanted to stick to 6mm wide sides and heat set inserts would not work with that.

I've done the Basic Shell with the Bottom, Sides, And Plate.

## Bottom
With the Bottom I used 2.9mm holes as clearance for the M2.5 screws. And I added .2mm of extra distance in the center where the pcb rests.
![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1774834336654-hpsc4g.png)
![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1774834338463-gsktvc.png)

## Sides
For the Sides I decided on 6mm Thickness, Not too Thick, and its enough for the Plastic. These also use 2.9mm Clearance holes.

![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1774834372655-iks0x3.png)

## Top
For the Plate I used [ai03 Plate Generator](https://kbplate.ai03.com/) to generate it, then I manually added the hole for the Rotary encoder.
![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1774836087596-solkto.png)
![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1774836090680-mlahpb.png)
The plate uses 2.2mm holes so that the M2.5 screws thread into it. For now I added 3mm on the sides on top of the Plate but that may change.
