I have planned out and made the start of the schematic for the key matrix. I used this 80% keyboard reference to help me understand and plan it.
![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1773919742843-iutj73.png)
The blue lines are the rows and the red lines are the columns.

Once I had the matrix drafted out, I put it into KiCad. I laid out the Rows and the Columns, then I placed the switches in the correct positions (With the Diodes).
![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1773920029648-xc9upg.png)
I know that I maybe could've optimised it to use less pins, but as of now I have no reason to minimize the amount of used pins and it would make the layout a lot harder to read.

Previously when I have made a matrix, I have edited the value of each switch to its respective key. e.g. SW -> Esc. But this time I made an embarassing mistake and edited the Reference instead of the value, I then had to go through all of the switches and correct my mistake.
